# Privacy Policy for Ant's Finance Tracker

**Last Updated:** [11/16/25]

## Introduction

Ant's Finance Tracker ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Offline-First Architecture

### 1.1 No Internet Required
The App is designed to work completely offline:
- No internet connection is required for core functionality
- No data synchronization with external servers
- All calculations and visualizations occur locally

### 1.2 Network Permissions
The App may request network permissions only for:
- Opening external websites when you click links (optional feature)
- These permissions are not required for core app functionality

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Financial Data
The App allows you to import and manage your personal financial data, including:
- Bank account information and balances
- Income sources and amounts
- Expense categories and amounts
- Debt information and repayment schedules
- Promotional financing details

**Important:** All financial data is provided directly by you through CSV file import. We do not collect this data automatically or from any external sources.

### 2.2 App Preferences
The App stores local preferences on your device, including:
- Dashboard tile arrangement and order
- User interface preferences

### 2.3 Technical Information
The App may collect minimal technical information necessary for app functionality:
- Device storage access (for reading and writing CSV files)
- File system access (for importing and exporting data)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Local Processing Only
All data processing occurs entirely on your device. Your financial data is:
- Processed locally for visualization and calculations
- Stored only on your device
- Never transmitted to external servers
- Never shared with third parties

### 3.2 App Functionality
We use your information solely to:
- Display financial visualizations and charts
- Calculate financial metrics (debt-to-credit ratios, expense-to-income ratios, etc.)
- Generate year-long financial projections
- Store your dashboard preferences

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- All financial data is stored locally on your device
- CSV files you import remain on your device
- App preferences are stored using device-local storage (SharedPreferences)
- No data is stored on external servers or cloud services

### 4.2 Data Security
- The App operates completely offline
- No network connectivity is required for core functionality
- Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it
- We do not have access to your data

### 4.3 Data Retention
- Your data remains on your device until you delete it
- You can delete the app at any time, which removes all locally stored data
- Exported CSV files are under your control and stored where you choose

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 No Automatic Sharing
We do not:
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your data
- Use your data for advertising
- Transmit your data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking services

### 5.2 User-Initiated Sharing
You may choose to:
- Export CSV files containing your data
- Share CSV templates with others
- Open external websites (e.g., sankeymatic.com) through links in the app

When you export or share data, you are responsible for how that data is used and protected.

## 6. Third-Party Services

### 6.1 External Links
The App may contain links to external websites (such as sankeymatic.com). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

### 6.2 No Third-Party Data Processors
The App does not use:
- Cloud storage services
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting services
- User tracking services

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Access to Your Data
- You have full access to all data stored in the App
- You can export your data at any time via CSV export
- You can view and modify your data through the App interface

### 7.2 Deletion of Data
- You can delete imported data by removing CSV files
- Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data
- You can clear app preferences through device settings

### 7.3 Data Portability
- All data can be exported in CSV format
- You maintain full control over your data

## 8. Data Minimization

We follow the principle of data minimization:
- We only collect data that you explicitly provide
- We only store data necessary for app functionality
- We do not collect unnecessary metadata or analytics
- We do not track user behavior

## 9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 10. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Google Play Store requirements
- Other applicable privacy laws

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 12. Consent

By using Ant's Finance Tracker, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

---

**Summary:** Ant's Finance Tracker is a privacy-first application that processes all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your financial data with any external parties. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.
